IMMUKI ISLAND:NO JUAN IS AN ISLAND

La Union is known for its crystal clear waters which attracts tourists from different regions. As you notice the formation of the island, its shape is being compared to the shape of a female genital originating the name of the island "IMMUKI". It is a word which was derived from a sea creature that can thrive only on the paringit (dead corals)-these are the SEA SLUGS OR BABAO or BAO in Panagasinense term which refers to the female genital or in Iloko "UKI". This place was being featured by one of the CKCian students, named Kyla Cyrine Avelino-BUKAL Feature page. it is being describe as an amazing experience because of the positive vibes it gives to all the tourists. This is located at Barangay Paraoir, Bacnotan, La Union.

Truly, no JUAN is an island because the people in that place will not let you feel away from your friends, family and your home because of the hospitable traits they possess. Like the Hundred Islands from the south, you will feel exactly the same as you try this awesome place with its paradise-like image. Filled with mangroove, the fishes live in the lagoon making your sight-seeing more enjoyable. You may wonder about the location but you can call the Local Tourism Council of La Union for directory because they are the best people who will direct you to this one of a kind experience from the hiddenparadise of La Union and be updated..........
